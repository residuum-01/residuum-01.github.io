What is Ergo?
Ergo is a Proof of Work Blockchain that draws on ten years of blockchain development, complementing tried and tested principles with the best peer-reviewed academic research into cryptography, consensus models and digital currencies.

Learn more at ergoplatform.org
What is the Ergo Auctionhouse?
The Ergo Auction House is a decentralized auction house, secure and easy to use. A simple way to sell or buy Ergo's tokens, artworks, NFTs, etc.
